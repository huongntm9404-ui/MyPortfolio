Pet Shop Mobile Application

University Team Project
Context: This project was developed as a university team assignment to simulate a real-world e-commerce product scenario, focusing on both customer and admin experiences in a pet shop ecosystem. The goal was to understand how users interact with product browsing, ordering, and management flows in a multi-role system.
Problem: Pet owners often need to switch between multiple channels to browse products, place orders, and manage pet-related purchases.
Solution: Designed a digital pet shop platform that allows customers to browse products, place orders, and track purchases while providing administrators with management tools.

My Contributions

  • Participated in team discussions to define core features for both the customer app and the admin management system
  • Contributed ideas to optimize shopping and ordering flows for a better user experience
  • Sketched initial wireframes and designed basic mockups on Figma for key user screens
  • Collaborated with team members during development to clarify functional behavior
  • Performed manual testing to ensure features matched expected product logic
